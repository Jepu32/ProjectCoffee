OUR VISION

Our cafe offers high-quality, well-chosen and well-studied coffees.

We prefer local food and products from small-scale producers. With the best coffee in the city we also offer small sweet and salty delicacies which all are produced within a radius of less than 10 km.

Our priority is to act responsibly and environmentally friendly.




THE ROASTERY

Our roastery staff currently consists of four employees who produce over 3000 kg of roasted coffee weekly.

There is also a shop where you can buy beans or coffee of choosing at our cafe or roastery.

Our high quality coffee and future product development is guaranteed by constantly testing and experimenting with new ways.

Our staff works hard and passionately which guarantees the best flavors and products.
